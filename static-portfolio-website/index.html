My Process

It is collaborative, iterative and driven by intent. It's called The Design Method. I hold various checkpoints during my process, presenting progress for review before each phase so I make sure that there are no surprises at the end.


Discovery

This is when the project kicks off. I work with you to understand what you are trying to achieve and what (good or bad) keeps you up at night. During the discovery process, I acquire data and insight about your business. This is no place for imagination (yet). This is about being pragmatic and fully understanding your unique situation.

Planning

Time to sit down and pull all the pieces together. Here we decide what platform, channels or technologies are appropriate for your challenges. Then develop a strategy or action plan, which will take us to the next phases. For visual references, I deliver this in a mood board and presentation, or as a wireframe or storyboard. I use these tools to discuss the mood of the project and engage in constructive conversations with you.

Creating

The exciting part starts. During the creative phase, I start sketching and looking for possible paths to reach a project's objective. I use all of the information gathered up until now to push our focus in one direction towards a final concept.

Application

Wheels up, lift off. I'll deliver the project concept or strategy, and walk you through the rationale and process it took to achieve this direction. Together, we'll discuss your questions and feedback. Once we agree on the proposed way forward, we confirm on the best implementation plan.
